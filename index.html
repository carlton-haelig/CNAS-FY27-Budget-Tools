Department of Defense
Budget Analysis Suite

Tools for analyzing DoD procurement and investment trends, from FY1970 historical topline data through the FY2027 President's Budget request. Built on P-1 exhibit data and DoD Green Book TOA figures.

Tools
P-1 Procurement Comparison
Compare procurement funding across FY2025 enacted, FY2026 enacted (discretionary + mandatory), and FY2027 request. Drill from service totals down to individual program lines with quantity data. Values displayed in millions (under $1B) or billions.
Open tool
Production & Delivery
Program-level production and delivery data for key munitions and interceptors. Covers funding history (FY2024–FY2027), lot-by-lot contract and delivery schedules, annual delivery totals, and production rate parameters. Sources: FY2027 P-1 (funding) and FY2026 service J-books (lots and deliveries).
Open tool
Historical Budget Trends
Long-run DoD budget trends from FY1970 to FY2026 in constant dollars. Covers topline spending, service-level breakdowns, RDT&E and procurement by title, and capability-area investment trends. Includes the RDT&E:Procurement ratio tracker from Stuck in the Cul-de-Sac (CNAS, 2025).
Open tool
AMD Cost, Production & Delivery
System-level and munitions-level data for U.S. air and missile defense programs. Covers 11 AMD systems (Patriot, THAAD, NASAMS, IFPC, M-SHORAD, FS-LIDS, M-LIDS, MADIS, LPWS, MRIC, alternative defeat systems) and 9 munitions programs with full procurement funding, lot history, delivery schedules, and production rates.
Open tool
Framework Milestones Tracker
Tracks the Pentagon's munitions framework deals from announcement through contract conversion. Covers 8 prime-level deals (PAC-3 MSE, THAAD, Tomahawk, AMRAAM, SM-6, SM-3 IIA, SM-3 IB, PrSM) and 4 sub-prime and industrial base actions. Compares FY27 P-1 requested quantities against announced production rate targets. Status field is live-editable as deals convert.
Open tool
AMD Cost Exchange & Optimization
Free-form scenario modeler for blue vs. red cost exchange analysis. Build a threat package (ballistic missiles, cruise missiles, UAS by group), configure blue interceptors with magazine depth, shot doctrine, and Pk by threat type, then run an optimized intercept laydown. Outputs cost exchange ratio, per-defeat cost, leaker count, and inventory depletion. Tradeoff slider adjusts between cost minimization and Pk maximization.
Open tool
Data Sources & Notes
P-1 Source
DoD Procurement Programs (P-1) Exhibit, FY2027 President's Budget, April 2026
P-1 Dollar Units
Displayed in millions (values under $1B) or billions, to two decimal places. Source data in thousands as reported in P-1 exhibit. Not inflation-adjusted.
Production & Delivery Sources
Funding: FY2027 P-1 Exhibit (authoritative). Lot data and delivery schedules: FY2026 service J-books (MDA Vol. 2B; Army MPA; Navy WPN; USMC PMC; AF MPA). FY2027 J-books not yet available.
AMD Overview Sources
System cost and inventory data: FY2026 service J-books and open-source secondary sources. Munitions data: FY2027 P-1 (funding) and FY2026 J-books (lot and delivery data). System narrative: CNAS AMD Cost Overview v2 (internal). Some system costs (LPWS, NINJA, FS-LIDS domestic) derived from secondary or FMS case data only.
Historical Source
DoD Green Book (National Defense Budget Estimates), Table 6-1 TOA by title
Constant Dollar Base
FY2026 constant dollars. FY2025 and FY2026 deflators are equivalent per DoD methodology (FY2026 deflators not released at time of data collection)
